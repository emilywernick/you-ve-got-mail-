Dear Liz,

Thank you for your postcard, it warmed my heart to hear from you! I was unable to find my stamps or any envelopes, so you get a visual letter instead (post offices here are a nightmare). I am happy to hear about your new job! What is it? I hope it offers you more comfort than your previous one.

It has finally become spring here. It was pretty chilly (20-30s) and randomly became 75 degree days. I think I like winter here more than I had previously assumed. Something about walking around in the snow, all bundled up, and barely any people outside. A special type of magic. My mom told me its been hot in the bay, I hope its been treating you well.

How are you and your partner? How has this new job been treating you? What books have you been reading recently? I reread Sarahland this week, and I think you would enjoy it. I hope to hear from you soon and I will be buying stamps in the next month.... at some point... I never got your forwarding address, please send that along whenever you can.

With love,

Emily
